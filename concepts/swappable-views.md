# swappable-views

[[swappable-views]] represent a foundational shift in how we interact with information. By decoupling data from specific applications, we enable a more fluid, user-centric experience where any installed app can provide different [[user-created-item-views]] for a single data type. You might, for example, choose how to render a task list based on your current need, swapping between a calendar view or a Kanban board on the fly. This architecture relies heavily on the [[item-centric]] nature of the system, supporting [[universal-data-portability]] and allowing users to customize their [[fluid-workspaces]] to match their specific intent.
