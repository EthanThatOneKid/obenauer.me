[[representation]] is the bridge between raw data and human cognition. It is how we manifest [[items]] within an [[interfaces|interface]], enabling [[views]] that allow us to interact with the underlying [[graph]] of our digital lives. By decoupling how data is stored from how it is perceived, we unlock true [[context]], transforming stagnant [[assets]] into dynamic interfaces we think with.

> [!idea] 
> The future of [[computing]] depends on flexible [[representation]]: enabling users to re-sculpt their digital environment to suit the fluid nature of human curiosity.

Ultimately, a thoughtful [[representation]] layer serves as a catalyst for [[user-agency]], moving beyond rigid application silos into a cohesive, itemized OS.
