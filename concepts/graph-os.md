The ==[[graph-os]]== is a vision of an operating system where the
[[items|item]]—rather than the application—is the primary unit of
[[interaction]]. By treating emails, [[tasks]], calendar events, and media as
interconnected nodes, the system creates an emergent, global [[graph]] of one's
digital life. This [[architecture]] transcends the silos of modern software,
enabling [[fluid-workspaces]] and [[contextual-notifications]] where any piece
of data can be referenced or transcluded into another. It is a fundamental shift
toward an environment designed for human [[thinking]] and [[discovery]],
allowing users to navigate through their information using meaningful
[[relationships]] instead of rigid, pre-defined hierarchies.

> [!idea] The power of the [[graph|Graph]] OS lies in its ability to let you
> point a reference directly to the exact information you need, regardless of
> where it lives or what app created it.
