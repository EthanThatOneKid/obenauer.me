[[time]] is the fundamental, linear scaffold of human experience, serving as an organizing principle for our [[document-environment]] and memories. While rooted in literal, planetary cycles, our perception of it is inherently elastic—a [[human-interpretation]] where moments stretch, compress, and carry varying weights. When we [[design|design]] a [[graph-os]] or a [[malleable-software]] ecosystem, we must move beyond rigid timestamps to honor this fluid, associative nature, grounding our digital [[interfaces|interface]] in the ways we actually dwell within and remember the passing of our lives.
