# [[actions]]

[[actions]] serve as the connective tissue between our intent and our data,
functioning as the verbs that transform passive [[items]] into dynamic
participants in our digital lives. By decoupling what can be done from the
specific application currently in use, we restore ==genuine user [[agency]]==,
allowing the system to surface relevant capabilities based on the [[context]] of
the current work rather than rigid app boundaries. This shift is essential for
moving toward an [[items|itemized-os]] where software adapts to our needs, not
the other way around.

> [!idea] [[actions|Actions]] are not merely commands; they are the
> [[interfaces|interface]] through which we manifest our [[thinking]] into the
> digital world, enabling a more seamless, fluid relationship with our tools.

Ultimately, by integrating [[services]] and [[automation|automations]] with
these atomic units of capability, we move beyond the siloed application model
toward an environment of true [[interoperability]], where every action feels
like a natural extension of our own cognitive reach.
