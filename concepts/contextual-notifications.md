[[contextual-notifications]] represent a reimagining of how we stay informed
within our digital environment, shifting away from interruptive alerts toward a
model where ==information lives within our periphery==. By integrating with
[[live-items]], these notifications allow us to track updates to the information
that matters most without breaking our flow. They function as a bridge between
active work and awareness, relying on [[services]] to surface relevant changes
directly into our workspace, transforming how we maintain [[context]] across our
[[fluid-workspaces]].

> [!idea] Instead of ephemeral alerts, contextual notifications treat updates as
> persistent, stateful [[entities]] that exist alongside our work, allowing us
> to choose when and how we engage with new information.

By tethering notifications to the [[items|item]] being updated, we ensure that
every update has inherent meaning and direct utility, moving us closer to a
future of more deliberate and focused
[[personal-computing|personal-computing-redefined]].
