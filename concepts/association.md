# [[association|association]]

[[association|Association]] is the cognitive glue of our digital lives. By recognizing how we move through [[paths]], the system can understand the inherent [[relationships|relationships]] between disparate [[items]], effectively bridging the gap between isolated data. This allows us to keep related information close, transcending the rigid file-system silos that currently fracture our [[context]].

> [!idea] The system as an active participant
> By observing our intent through movement, the system can autonomously surface relevant information, often without us ever needing to explicitly ==declare that [[association|association]]==.

Through deliberate, manual management or passive, autonomous observation, we create a fluid [[graph]] of our intellectual work. This shift moves [[computing|computing]] away from static storage toward a responsive environment that understands what we need, when we need it, based on the threads of our own [[thinking]].
