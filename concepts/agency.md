# agency

# [[agency]]

[[agency]] is the fundamental capacity for a person to act with intention and effect within their digital environment, inextricably linked to [[malleable-software]], [[personal-computing]], and [[user-intent]]. It is not an abstract feature, but an empirical outcome of interfaces that serve the individual rather than the platform. 

> [!idea] 
> True ==agency== exists only when the tools we use are as malleable as our thoughts, allowing us to shape our computing experience to match the contours of our own lives.

Without a grounding in specific, lived-in contexts, [[agency]] remains hollow. By rethinking the relationship between human and machine, we can reclaim our ability to explore, create, and think freely through the tools we choose to inhabit.
