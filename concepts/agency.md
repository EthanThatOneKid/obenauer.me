# [[agency]]

[[user-agency]] is the capacity to mold our digital environments to match the
unique, evolving rhythms of our lives, rather than bending our own [[thinking]]
to fit the rigid, static constraints of current software. True [[agency]]
emerges when we move away from monolithic applications toward
[[malleable-software]] that respects our [[context]] and allows for personal
[[navigation]] through our own information. By prioritizing
[[interfaces|interfaces-with-which-we-think]], we reclaim the power to shape our
tools into personal extensions of our cognition.

> [!idea] Software should be a supportive partner, not a static cage; when we
> gain control over our [[views]] and information [[architecture|structure]], we
> move from being passive consumers to active architects of our digital
> experience.

> [!important] ==[[agency|Agency]] is the rejection of rigid [[design]] in favor
> of systems that evolve alongside our personal growth and shifting needs.==
