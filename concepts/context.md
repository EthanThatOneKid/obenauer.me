# [[context]]

[[context|Context]] is the lived environment of our digital work, a fluid space that replaces the fragmented reality of window-management. By breaking [[items]] out of the rigid containers of traditional applications, we enable a more natural flow of [[thinking]] and creation, where information is no longer trapped but available to move freely across your workspace. This requires reimagining [[computing]] as an experience centered on the individual, where [[navigation]] follows the organic [[paths]] of your activity rather than the limitations of software silos.

> [!idea] 
> True digital [[agency]] emerges when we move away from flipping through [[windows]] and toward creating persistent, meaningful spaces—your [[context|contexts]]—where the tools and information you need are always right where you left them.

==[[context|Context]] allows us to transcend the app-centric paradigm==, fostering a deeper, more intentional relationship with the information we navigate daily.
