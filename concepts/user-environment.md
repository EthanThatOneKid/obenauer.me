# user-environment

A user-environment is the experimental digital landscape where we redefine how software serves the individual. By moving away from siloed applications toward an [[items|itemization]]-first [[architecture|architecture]], we shift the focus from managing files to nurturing ==human-centric workflows==. This system acts as a living, [[malleable-software]] space that prioritizes personal [[agency]] and [[creativity]] over static constraints, ensuring every [[interaction|interaction]] contributes to the whole.

> [!idea]
> When we treat the user-environment as a cohesive, itemized system, every new feature becomes a building block for the entire experience rather than just a siloed tool.

This approach draws upon [[personal-computing]] [[research|research]] to foster deeper curiosity and broader [[opportunity]] in our daily digital interactions.
