[[personal-computing|personal-computing-redefined]] is an active endeavor to construct the next phase of life for our digital tools, ensuring they serve as extensions of our human intent rather than adversaries. It requires a deep engagement with [[bootstrapping]], examining the layers of our technical history to build future-proof systems that prioritize [[agency]], [[creativity]], and [[curiosity]]. By centering the user, we move beyond mere utility to craft environments that fundamentally support the cognitive work of living.

> [!idea]
> The goal is to move beyond the constraints of our current era, crafting a [[tool-making-culture]] where [[computing]] acts as a true medium for thought.

Ultimately, this is about ensuring that the systems we inhabit empower, rather than diminish, our capacity to think and create.
