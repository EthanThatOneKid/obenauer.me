An **[[item|item]]** is the fundamental, atomic unit of information within a personal [[computing]] [[domain|domain]], standing independent of its specific source or format. By utilizing [[transclusion]] to embed these [[items|items]] within one another, we dissolve the rigid boundaries between disparate files and applications. These [[items|items]] exist as nodes within a sprawling [[items-graph]], linked by [[references|references]] that capture the nuance of our [[thinking]]. Through [[paths]], [[items|items]] are organized into fluid, temporal browsing [[contexts|contexts]], allowing us to interact with our work as a continuous, side-by-side flow rather than isolated silos.

> [!idea] 
> The power of an [[item|item]] lies in its malleability; by allowing developers to create or swap custom [[views|views]], we move from passive consumption of data to an active, tailored [[interface|interface]] for our own cognition.

> [!important] 
> [[items|Items]] should be retrieved through [[association|association]], search, or creation within a [[context|context]], ensuring that our digital tools support our natural mental processes rather than forcing us to conform to the system's limitations.
