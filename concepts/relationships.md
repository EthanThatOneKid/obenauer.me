[[relationships|Relationships]] represent the connective tissue of our digital
environments, moving beyond siloed files toward a fluid [[graph]] of [[items]].
By prioritizing these associations, we shift from managing static containers to
nurturing dynamic [[context|contexts]] that mirror how our minds actually weave
information together. True [[computing]] power resides not in the [[items]]
themselves, but in the ==rich, explicit connections== we build between them,
allowing for more intuitive [[representation]] of our knowledge and daily
workflows.
