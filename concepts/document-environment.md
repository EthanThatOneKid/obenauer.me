A ==document-environment== is a fundamental shift toward an itemized [[interfaces|interface]], moving away from rigid application silos toward a fluid, graph-based experience. By treating information as distinct, addressable [[items|items]], we enhance [[agency]] over our digital lives, enabling [[creativity]] to flourish through seamless [[universal-data-portability|data-portability]] and interconnected workflows. 

> [!note] Core Vision
> The document-environment serves as the substrate for a new [[computing]] paradigm, where the boundaries between "files" and "[[apps|apps]]" dissolve in favor of a cohesive, user-centric landscape.

> [!idea] Future Outlook
> This approach treats every piece of data as a first-class citizen, allowing for spontaneous [[discovery|discovery]] and deeper [[curiosity]] as we navigate our knowledge as a persistent, evolving [[graph|graph]] rather than static, disconnected folders.
