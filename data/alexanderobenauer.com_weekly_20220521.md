# The future of personal computing software

We’ve explored lots of possible features for the OS of the future. But how
should systems of the future reify their functionality? How should they make
their primitives available to users? Who has the ability to evolve these systems
long into the future?

This week’s lab note considers how flexible systems could work for users of all
levels of computer literacy:

[LN 028: Designing systems for computer literacy and evolvability »](https://alexanderobenauer.com/labnotes/028/)
