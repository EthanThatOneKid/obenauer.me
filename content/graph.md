A ==graph== is a way to structure thought by writing discrete, deeply linked
[[item|items]]. By weaving connections through internal links and backlinks, we
form neighborhoods of understanding, allowing [[knowledge]] to grow organically
rather than in [[silo|silos]]. Imagine if the entire [[os]] functioned as this
interconnected fabric—where every individual digital thing could be referenced,
enabling a fluid, [[context]]-rich [[environment]] for our work.

> [!idea] The ultimate evolution of [[computing]] is a [[system]] where the
> boundaries between disparate pieces of information dissolve into a singular,
> traversable, and highly [[personal]] digital map.
