True ==storage== is not a static warehouse for bits; it is the living substrate of our [[sovereignty]]. By moving beyond file-based silos, we transform information into a fluid, [[localfirst]] ecosystem where [[items]] exist independently of specific [[apps]]. This transition empowers us to reclaim our digital artifacts as personal assets, ensuring our memory and [[workflow]] remain malleable, accessible, and truly ours.

> [!idea] Storage must be viewed as an extension of our own cognition, not a bureaucratic necessity of the operating system.

> [!important] When we decouple data from rigid containers, we invite a future where our digital environment mirrors the organic interconnectedness of our own thinking.
