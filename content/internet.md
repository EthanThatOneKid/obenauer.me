The [[internet]] is the digital substrate of our modern existence, functioning as the vast, interconnected [[environment]] where our [[computing]] tools reside. It is not merely a transport layer but a foundational [[architecture]] that must be reimagined to foster human [[sovereignty]] and [[agency]]. By transitioning from rigid, siloed experiences toward a more [[malleable]] and [[itemcentric]] paradigm, we can transform the network from a space of fragmented [[data]] into a coherent, [[interoperable]] fabric that supports our daily [[thinking]].

> [!idea]
> The true potential of the internet is unlocked when we treat it as an extension of our own cognitive reach, allowing for seamless [[linking]] and [[discovery]] across distributed systems.

We must move past the current limitations of the [[cloud]] to ensure our digital [[assets]] remain within our personal control, prioritizing [[localfirst]] access while maintaining global connectivity. The [[future]] relies on this shift: replacing isolated [[apps]] with a fluid, unified [[workspace]] where ideas flow without friction.
