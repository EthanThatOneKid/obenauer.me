[[zoom]] is the fluid, gestural adaptation of content presentation based on
available display space, shifting how information is revealed as a user expands
or contracts an element. By moving beyond rigid, pre-packaged [[interface]]
boundaries, it grants the user [[agency]] to determine their own vantage point
within an [[environment]]. As a core mechanism in [[computing]], it facilitates
a seamless transition—such as an [[email]]'s compact preview transforming into a
fully-rendered [[document]]—ensuring that information remains meaningful,
legible, and responsive to the user's immediate [[thinking]] needs.
