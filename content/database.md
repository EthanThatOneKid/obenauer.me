> [!idea] 
> A ==database== is not a static silo but a fluid foundation for [[computing]] and [[agency]]. It shifts the paradigm from rigid file management to an [[itemized]] flow of information, where granular [[units]] of thought remain accessible, portable, and inherently linked within the [[environment]]. By treating every bit of data as a first-class citizen, we transcend the limitations of traditional [[apps]] and move toward a [[sovereignty]] that mirrors the way we actually think—interconnected, evolving, and contextual.

> [!note] 
> To truly serve human life, the database must be a substrate for [[creativity]], enabling us to weave disparate pieces of knowledge into a coherent tapestry, rather than locking them away in disconnected containers.
