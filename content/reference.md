A [[reference]] acts as the [[fundamental]] connective tissue for our digital
existence, allowing us to transcend the [[silo|silos]] of traditional
[[software]] to organize our lives as a unified whole. By treating every digital
object as an [[item]], [[reference|references]] become the mechanism that
enables fluid [[navigation]], ensuring our [[environment]] remains responsive to
our unique patterns of [[thinking]].

> [!idea] Think of [[reference|references]] not as static links, but as dynamic
> entry points into a cohesive [[system]] where every [[item]] is aware of its
> [[context]].

Ultimately, by centering our [[interface]] on these connections, we regain
[[agency]] over our own data, allowing for deeper exploration and more
meaningful associations between the disparate threads of our work and lives.
