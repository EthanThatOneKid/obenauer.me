[[interoperability]] is the antitoxin to digital [[silo|silos]], moving beyond
mere file formats to the [[fundamental]] ability for [[system|systems]] and data
to truly collaborate. It serves as a core enabler for [[computing]], where your
data acts as a fluid resource that flows, transforms, and combines across
[[software]] [[environment|environments]] without friction. By rejecting the
isolation inherent in traditional [[interface]] [[design]], we regain
[[agency]], ensuring that users—not vendors—dictate the flow of information and
the construction of their digital worlds.

> [!important] When [[tool|tools]] interoperate, your data is no longer
> imprisoned; it becomes a composable, intelligent [[workspace]], allowing for
> genuine cognitive [[augmentation]].

> [!idea] We must transition from isolated [[window|windows]] to a [[system]]
> where data [[item|items]], accompanied by [[view|views]] from their hosting
> apps, can be moved freely across our digital landscapes.
