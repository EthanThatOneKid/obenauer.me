[[fundamental]] are the essential building blocks of our digital experiences, representing the core primitives that define how we construct and interact with [[computing]]. By identifying these foundational elements, we create the groundwork for a more malleable, [[item|item-centric]] future where flexibility serves the user rather than complicating their [[workflow]]. We must continuously question: what are the elemental units that compose our systems, and how do they enable us to reclaim [[agency]] within our personal [[environment]]?
