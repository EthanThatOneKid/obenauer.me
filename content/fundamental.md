[[fundamental|fundamentals]] are the essential building blocks of our digital experiences, representing the core [[primitive|primitives]] that define how we construct and interact with [[computing]]. By identifying these foundational elements, we create the groundwork for a more malleable, [[item|item-centric]] [[future]] where flexibility serves the user rather than complicating their [[workflow]]. We must continuously question: what are the elemental [[unit|units]] that compose our [[system|systems]], and how do they enable us to reclaim [[agency]] within our [[personal]] [[environment]]?
