> [!note] paths
> ==Paths== represent the navigable sequences and intentional journeys through our digital workspace. By shifting focus from static locations to active movement, we transform the [[environment]] into a living structure where our [[intent]] guides our discovery. These journeys are deeply intertwined with the [[graph]] of our [[items]], allowing us to trace meaningful connections between disparate [[assets]] while maintaining the fluidity required for true [[cognition]].

> [!idea]
> Think of paths not as rigid directories, but as fluid, user-defined trajectories that connect [[context]], [[linking]], and [[navigation]]. When we embrace paths, we move beyond simple file access and into a space of active [[experimentation]] and fluid [[composition]].
