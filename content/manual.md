# manual

In our current era of over-[[automation]], the act of being ==manual== is a radical reclamation of [[agency]]. It is the deliberate choice to participate directly in the [[creation]] of our own [[thinking]] and [[thinking]] [[process|processes]] rather than delegating them to black-box [[software]]. By engaging in manual [[interaction]], we maintain a tactile connection to the [[malleability]] of our digital [[environment]], ensuring that our [[workspace]] remain extensions of our own [[intent]] rather than rigid, prescribed paths.

> [!note]
> Being manual is the antidote to the passive consumption of utility; it is the craftsman’s way of ensuring the [[system]] serves the user, not the inverse.

By prioritizing manual control, we preserve our [[sovereignty]] over the digital [[asset]] we produce. This approach allows us to iterate with genuine [[creativity]], moving away from automated [[silo|silos]] and toward a [[future]] where our [[tool]] are truly transparent and open to our ongoing [[experimentation]].
