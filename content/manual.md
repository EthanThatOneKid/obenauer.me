# manual

In our current era of over-automation, the act of being ==manual== is a radical reclamation of [[agency]]. It is the deliberate choice to participate directly in the creation of our own [[cognition]] and [[thinking]] processes rather than delegating them to black-box [[software]]. By engaging in manual [[interaction]], we maintain a tactile connection to the [[malleability]] of our digital [[environment]], ensuring that our [[workspaces]] remain extensions of our own intent rather than rigid, prescribed paths.

> [!note]
> Being manual is the antidote to the passive consumption of utility; it is the craftsman’s way of ensuring the system serves the user, not the inverse.

By prioritizing manual control, we preserve our [[sovereignty]] over the digital [[assets]] we produce. This approach allows us to iterate with genuine [[creativity]], moving away from automated silos and toward a future where our [[tools]] are truly transparent and open to our ongoing [[experimentation]].
