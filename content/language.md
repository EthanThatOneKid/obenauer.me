[[language|Language]] is the [[fundamental]] medium through which we exert [[intent]]
upon the digital [[environment]]. It serves as the bridge between human
[[thinking]] and machine [[logic]], transforming abstract thoughts into
actionable structure. By refining the [[representation]] of our ideas, we move
beyond static [[silo|silos]] toward a more fluid, malleable [[relationship]]
with the [[tool|tools]] we inhabit.

> [!idea] True digital [[literacy]] isn't just about reading code; it's about
> ==expressing our intentions directly== into the [[system|systems]] we use to
> think.

When we formalize our language, we move from mere [[interaction]] toward genuine
[[symbiosis]], ensuring that our digital [[tool|tools]] act as extensions of our
own creative capacity rather than rigid constraints.
