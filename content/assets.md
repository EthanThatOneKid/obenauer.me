# assets

Assets are the fundamental, atomic units of our digital existence, serving as the raw material for our [[cognition]] and [[creativity]]. By treating digital objects as first-class citizens, we move away from restrictive app silos toward an [[itemcentric]] paradigm where [[thinking]] is supported by the direct manipulation of our own creations. These assets are not mere data; they are the tangible extensions of our intent within an [[environment]] designed for fluid interaction.

> [!idea]
> When we prioritize the fluidity of assets over the rigid boundaries of software, we reclaim our [[agency]], turning the computer from an adversary into a creative partner.

==Truly powerful computing happens when assets move seamlessly between contexts,== allowing our ideas to evolve through continuous [[experimentation]].
