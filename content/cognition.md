Cognition in the digital realm is not merely biological; it is an active exploration of the types of ==thinking native to digital environments==. We are iterating toward a deeper understanding of how our tools shape our capacity for thought, constantly questioning if we have yet found the right [[interface]] to augment our limited physical frames. By studying [[computing]], we seek to bridge the gap between human curiosity and the vast potential for [[creativity]] latent within our machines.

> [!idea] Cognition is an iterative process. We are slowly unfolding what it means to think alongside our tools, moving beyond the physical to discover new modes of expression.

We must remain curious as we observe the digital landscape, recognizing that our current tools are just the beginning of a larger evolution in how we process complexity.
