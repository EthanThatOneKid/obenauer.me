To be [[itemized]] is to embrace a philosophy where all digital entities—notes, emails, tasks—are treated as discrete, uniform [[items]]. This shift demands that we move beyond monolithic applications toward a fluid [[environment]] where information is decoupled from rigid silos, enabling seamless [[composition]] and [[integration]].

> [!note] The Power of the Unit
> By treating every digital object as a standard building block, we unlock the ability for any [[view]] to render any [[item]]. This malleability turns our computer from a collection of apps into a cohesive, personalized workspace.

Everything is data, waiting to be linked, nested, or rearranged to serve human intent. In this vision, we do not just store files; we cultivate a living, evolving structure of interconnected thoughts and actions.
