A [[corpus]] represents the total body of digitized thought—the unified collection of [[items]] that form our personal and collective knowledge base. It is not merely a storage container but an active [[environment]] where [[data]] becomes alive through [[linking]], [[context]], and [[navigation]]. By treating the entirety of one's [[notes]] as a single, searchable, and malleable entity, we move beyond the fragmentation of traditional [[apps]] toward a more sovereign [[architecture]] of information. 

> [!note] 
> The corpus is the living foundation upon which we build, allowing for deeper [[discovery]] and more meaningful [[composition]]. 

> [!idea] 
> When information is treated as a cohesive whole rather than isolated silos, we enable true [[augmentation]] of human [[cognition]].
