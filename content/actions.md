# [[actions]]

[[actions]] serve as the connective tissue between our [[intent|intent]] and our data,
functioning as the verbs that transform passive [[items]] into dynamic
participants in our digital lives. By decoupling what can be done from the
specific application currently in use, we restore ==genuine user [[agency]]==,
allowing the system to surface relevant capabilities based on the [[context]] of
the current work rather than rigid app boundaries. This shift is essential for
moving toward an [[items]] where software adapts to our needs, not the other way
around.

> [!idea] [[actions]] are not merely commands; they are the [[interfaces]]
> through which we manifest our [[thinking]] into the digital world, enabling a
> more seamless, fluid relationship with our [[tools|tools]].

Ultimately, by integrating [[services]] and [[automation]] with these atomic
[[units|units]] of capability, we move beyond the siloed application model toward an
environment of true [[interoperability]], where every action feels like a
natural extension of our own cognitive reach.
