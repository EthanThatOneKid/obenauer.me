True ==universal [[reference]] containers== transcend the rigid, siloed apps of today, allowing for fluid [[portability]] across our digital lives. When we break free from these boundaries, we realize that all [[item]]—from [[note|notes]] and [[task|tasks]] to images—possess the inherent potential to act as a container for others. By integrating these [[relationship]] into a cohesive [[system]], we empower ourselves to compose [[workspace]] where data lives wherever we place it, shifting our [[interaction]] from fragmented [[task|tasks]] to unified [[thinking]].
