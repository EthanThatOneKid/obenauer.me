[[asset|Assets]] are the [[fundamental]], atomic [[unit|units]] of our digital existence, serving as the raw material for our [[thinking]] and [[creativity]]. By treating digital objects as first-class citizens, we move away from restrictive app [[silo|silos]] toward an [[item]] paradigm where [[thinking]] is supported by the direct manipulation of our own [[creation|creations]]. These [[asset|assets]] are not mere data; they are the tangible extensions of our [[intent]] within an [[environment]] designed for fluid [[interaction]].

> [!idea] When we prioritize the fluidity of [[asset|assets]] over the rigid boundaries of [[software]], we reclaim our [[agency]], turning the computer from an adversary into a creative partner.

==Truly powerful [[computing]] happens when [[asset|assets]] move seamlessly between [[context|contexts]],== allowing our ideas to evolve through continuous [[experimentation]].
