---
title: semantics
---

[[semantics]] refers to the meaningful arrangement and interpretation of data within an [[interface]], enabling [[system|systems]] to understand the essence of our information rather than just its structure. By embedding [[intent]] into the [[architecture]], we move beyond rigid, file-bound paradigms toward a state where [[interface]] elements adapt to the user's current [[context]]. Through techniques like [[zoom]], we can transition between abstract overviews and concrete details, fundamentally enhancing [[agency]] and allowing [[software]] to reflect the fluid, evolving nature of human thought.
