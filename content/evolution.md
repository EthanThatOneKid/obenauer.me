> [!important] Evolution is the deliberate capacity of a system to grow alongside its user, ensuring that our [[computing]] environments remain fluid rather than static. By prioritizing [[evolvability]], we move away from brittle, monolithic [[software]] that inhibits [[creativity]], favoring instead architectures that empower individual [[agency]].

> [!idea] To truly support the user, systems must be designed for longevity—allowing the [[architecture]] to shift, expand, and refine as our personal needs and understanding change over the [[timeline]] of our work.
