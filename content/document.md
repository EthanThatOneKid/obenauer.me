A [[document]] is not merely a static file, but a dynamic vessel for progress and a manifestation of working with the lab door open. By capturing the evolution of [[creativity]], [[curiosity]], and [[computing]] in real-time, it transforms isolated thoughts into an interconnected [[environment]] that invites collective participation.

> [!note]
> To document is to provide the raw, unpolished proof of discovery, ensuring our progress remains transparent, accessible, and grounded in the reality of the creative process.

Ultimately, a document serves as an anchor for [[agency]], allowing us to trace the [[timeline]] of our ideas and expand our capacity for meaningful impact within the digital landscape.
