A timeline is the foundational substrate through which our [[computing]] environments must perceive and represent the progression of our work. It is not merely a linear sequence, but a dynamic canvas for [[agency]] that allows us to interact with the past and future of our digital existence through [[interface]] mechanisms like [[zoom]].

> [!important]
> To truly support human cognition, our operating systems must treat time as a first-class citizen, moving beyond static file systems into fluid, temporal experiences.

By reimagining the [[timeline]], we transform how we navigate our creative process, ensuring that the software we build respects the organic, non-linear ways we think.
