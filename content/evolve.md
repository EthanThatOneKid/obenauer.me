To ==evolve== is to embrace a system’s inherent [[evolvability]], shifting away from rigid, pre-defined software towards a fluid landscape where users can directly manipulate their [[computing]] [[environment]]. This requires moving beyond black-box applications, favoring [[graph]]-based structures that invite the user to wander, discover, and refine their own [[tools]] through time. It is not about reaching a static perfection, but cultivating the capacity for a system to grow alongside the user’s changing [[intent]].

> [!idea]
> Evolvability is the bridge between a blank canvas and a mastery-oriented workspace; it allows a system to start simple while remaining infinitely capable of refinement.

By prioritizing user-driven [[navigation]] and the ability to reshape [[views]], we transform the machine from a static destination into a living participant in our [[cognition]].
