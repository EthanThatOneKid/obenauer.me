> [!note] Defining Tuples
> Tuples represent the foundational, ordered arrangements of data that underpin our [[system]], acting as the discrete packets of meaning that enable [[interoperability]] across domains. By treating information as structured ==tuples== rather than isolated files, we move toward a model of [[computing]] where every piece of knowledge is inherently linkable, queryable, and ready for [[composition]]. This shift transforms how we manage our digital life, allowing us to build [[context]] through precise [[relationships]] rather than being trapped in arbitrary [[silos]]. 

We must move past the limitations of current [[architecture]] to embrace a more granular, [[itemcentric]] approach. When we prioritize these atomic units, we unlock the potential for truly [[malleable]] software that respects user [[sovereignty]] and enhances our ability to navigate the vast [[graph]] of our personal thoughts and projects.
