Current [[apps]] are often siloed containers that hinder the fluid movement of our digital lives, conflicting with the ideal of a cohesive [[os]] that prioritizes user [[agency]]. We must reimagine these as flexible tools that prioritize [[itemization]] over rigid software boundaries, allowing us to interact with our digital [[environment]] in a way that respects human [[cognition]].

> [!idea] Shift in Perspective
> The goal is to move from managing applications to orchestrating meaningful ==actions== on digital items, shifting the focus from the tool to the task itself.

By breaking down these silos, we can foster deeper [[creativity]] and sustain our natural [[curiosity]], transforming how we work and play.
