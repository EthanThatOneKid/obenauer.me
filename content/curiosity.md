---
title: curiosity
---

Curiosity is the [[fundamental]] spark that [[drive|drives]] us to explore, learn, and contribute our own unique node into the [[graph]] of human [[knowledge]]. It is deeply connected to [[computing]], acting as a catalyst for expanding human [[agency]] and deepening our engagement with the [[tool|tools]] we use for [[interface]] [[design]] and creative expression. By cultivating an inquisitive mindset, we transform our [[interaction|interactions]] with [[software]] from passive consumption into active, meaningful exploration.

> [!note] Inquisitive thought work If we can help others engage in this kind of inquisitive thought work throughout all facets of life—whether it is music, nature, or the [[system|systems]] we build—we unlock the greatest potential for progress.

> [!idea] The Spark Curiosity is not merely a trait; it is a deliberate [[practice]] of looking for the unanswered questions that define our [[future]].
