# curiosity

Curiosity is the fundamental spark that drives us to explore, learn, and contribute our own unique node into the graph of human knowledge. It is deeply connected to [[computing]], acting as a catalyst for expanding human [[agency]] and deepening our engagement with the tools we use for [[interface]] design and creative expression. By cultivating an inquisitive mindset, we transform our interactions with [[software]] from passive consumption into active, meaningful exploration.

> [!note] Inquisitive thought work
> If we can help others engage in this kind of inquisitive thought work throughout all facets of life—whether it is music, nature, or the systems we build—we unlock the greatest potential for progress.

> [!idea] The Spark
> Curiosity is not merely a trait; it is a deliberate practice of looking for the unanswered questions that define our future.
