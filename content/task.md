[[task|Tasks]] represent the active manifestation of ==human [[intent]]==,
bridging the gap between [[personal]] cognition and [[system]]-level
[[action|actions]]. By integrating [[intent]] with [[item|items]] and
[[automation|automations]], [[task|tasks]] move beyond static lists, becoming
dynamic expressions of our [[agency]]. When a task is surfaced through the right
[[context]], it empowers us to transform [[project|projects]] into tangible
outcomes through a seamless, interconnected [[os]] [[architecture]].

> [!idea] Tasks as Flow A task is not merely a box to check; it is a point of
> synthesis where the user's focus meets the [[system]]'s [[malleability]],
> allowing [[automation]] to handle the procedural while we nurture the
> creative.
