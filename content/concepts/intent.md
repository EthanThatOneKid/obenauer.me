# user-intent

User-intent represents the bridge between human thought and digital execution.
It is the core of [[agency]], moving software from a passive tool into a
reactive [[environment]] that understands where you want to go. When we
prioritize [[intent|intent]], we move beyond rigid applications toward [[malleability]]
that adapts to our specific needs, fostering deeper [[creativity]] and
[[curiosity]].

> [!idea] [[intent|Intent]] as a First-Class Citizen By capturing the essence of what a
> person is trying to achieve, the system can provide the right [[context]] at
> the right time, rather than forcing the user to navigate the artificial
> barriers of modern interfaces.

Ultimately, designing for [[intent|intent]] is about ensuring that our [[computing]] [[tools|tools]]
serve the human, not the other way around.
