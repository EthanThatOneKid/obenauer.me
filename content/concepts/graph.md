The ==[[graph]]== is the connective tissue of a reimagined [[computing]]
environment, shifting the focus from rigid file hierarchies to a fluid,
interconnected space of [[items]]. By anchoring our digital lives in a
[[architecture]] where every piece of information exists as an entity capable of
forming explicit [[references]], we move away from silos and toward a cohesive,
navigable intelligence. This [[architecture]] empowers users to traverse their
own thoughts, surfacing [[context]] through dynamic [[views]] that treat
information as a living, breathing network rather than static data.
