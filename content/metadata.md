Metadata is the essential connective tissue of our digital [[environment]],
bridging the gap between raw [[item|items]] and meaningful [[context]]. By
providing structure to our [[thinking]] and digital artifacts, it allows for the
seamless graphing of disparate pieces of information, transforming isolated data
into a navigable [[graph]] of [[intent]]. It is the [[language]] through which
we describe the 'what' and 'why' of our digital lives, empowering users to move
beyond rigid structures toward a more fluid, malleable [[interaction]] with
their own work.
