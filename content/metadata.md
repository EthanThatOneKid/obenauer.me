---
title: metadata
---

Metadata is the essential connective tissue of our digital [[environment]], bridging the gap between raw [[item|items]] and meaningful [[context]]. By providing structure to our [[thinking|cognition]] and digital artifacts, it allows for the seamless linking of disparate pieces of information, transforming isolated data into a navigable [[graph]] of [[intent]]. It is the [[language]] through which we describe the 'what' and 'why' of our digital lives, empowering users to move beyond rigid structures toward a more fluid, [[malleability|malleable]] [[interaction]] with their own work.
