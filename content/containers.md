True ==universal reference containers== transcend the rigid, siloed apps of today, allowing for fluid [[portability]] across our digital lives. When we break free from these boundaries, we realize that all [[items]]—from notes and tasks to images—possess the inherent potential to act as a container for others. By integrating these [[relationships]] into a cohesive [[system]], we empower ourselves to compose [[workspaces]] where data lives wherever we place it, shifting our interaction from fragmented tasks to unified [[thinking]].
