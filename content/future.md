The [[future]] is not a destination, but a space for ==reimagining the user environment== through active [[research]] and consistent [[iteration]]. By confronting our initial, often myopic, [[thinking]]—those that feel impossible or uninteresting—we clear the path for genuine [[design]] breakthroughs. This work is an exercise in stripping away the constraints of current [[computing]] to focus on what tools actually do for us in our daily [[workflow]].

> [!idea]
> To build the future, one must be willing to let go of the "terrible" initial answers. Only by exhausting these limitations can we uncover the true [[sovereignty]] and [[malleability]] required for the next generation of personal [[os]].

It is about moving past kernels and drivers to cultivate a richer [[symbiosis]] between human intent and the digital [[environment]].
