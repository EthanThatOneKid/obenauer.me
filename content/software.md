# software

[[software]] is the digital architecture we inhabit, a foundational layer that dictates our daily [[computing]] experiences and the limits of our [[interface]]. It is not a static object but a living, malleable craft. To truly understand it, one must embrace the practice of rebuilding: creating, breaking, and iterating from scratch to uncover the core truths hidden within the complexity. By focusing on the basics and iterating through wild variations, we move past "good enough" to find the fundamental constructs that genuinely serve human agency.

> [!note] The act of building is a discovery process.
> You cannot find the best path forward without proving the wrong ones first. Quality is born from the discipline of rewriting, ensuring that the final foundation is built upon clear, understood primitives.

==We must build and rebuild==, not merely to produce, but to evolve our comprehension of how these digital tools can better support our lives, curiosity, and creativity.
