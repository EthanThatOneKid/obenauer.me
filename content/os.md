The [[os]] is the foundational substrate through which we interact with the digital realm, acting as a canvas for [[thinking]] and human [[agency]]. By embracing an [[item]] philosophy, we shift away from rigid applications toward a fluid [[environment]] where our work is organized as discrete, meaningful pieces rather than isolated files. This transition demands that we rethink the nature of the [[interface]]—moving beyond legacy metaphors to craft systems that prioritize [[creativity]], foster deep [[curiosity]], and honor the [[timeline]] of our intellectual growth.
