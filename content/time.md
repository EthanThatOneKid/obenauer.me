---
title: time
---

Our sense of [[time]] functions as a [[fundamental]], linear progression, serving as the essential scaffolding for our [[memory]], [[intent]], and [[planning]]. While solar cycles provide rigid anchors, our internal experience often ebbs and flows, with specific periods carrying varying emotional weight and associative depth. By grounding our digital [[environment]] in this human interpretation—rather than merely literal machine timestamps—we can create a more resonant [[workspace]] where [[item|items]] and [[project|projects]] naturally align with the human rhythm of [[thinking]].
