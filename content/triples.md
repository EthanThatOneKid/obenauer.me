# triples

Triples represent the fundamental ==atomic architecture of knowledge==, defining the relationships between entities in our [[computing]] environment. By connecting a subject, a predicate, and an object, we move beyond stagnant silos and into a fluid [[graph]] of [[data]].

> [!note] The Power of Connection
> When we model information as triples, we enable true [[interoperability]]. Every [[item]] becomes part of a larger, queryable fabric, transforming how we engage with [[logic]] and [[metadata]].

By leveraging this structure, we foster a [[malleable]] [[system]] where [[facts]] can be linked, discovered, and rendered through diverse [[views]], ensuring our digital tools finally reflect the interconnected nature of our [[thinking]].
