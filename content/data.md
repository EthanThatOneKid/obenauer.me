[[data]] is not merely a static collection of records; it is a living, breathing history of our intent and action. To truly empower human [[cognition]], we must shift from viewing information as opaque files to seeing the ==transformation of data as an important piece of data itself==. By treating changes and mutations as first-class citizens, we bridge the gap between our [[thinking]] and the [[os]] that supports it, ensuring our [[workspace]] remains a reflection of our evolving path.

> [!idea]
> When we capture the provenance of an idea—the 'how' and 'when' of its becoming—we reclaim the [[sovereignty]] lost to siloed systems.

We must move toward a model where every bit of [[metadata]] serves as a bridge, linking our past efforts to future [[creativity]]. Data becomes a fluid medium, subject to constant refinement and recontextualization within a unified [[architecture]].
