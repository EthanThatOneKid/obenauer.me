# collaboration

Collaboration is the act of thinking alongside other minds, a process where individual perspectives converge to create something greater than the sum of their parts. It thrives when users operate within a shared [[context]], riffing on each other’s [[ideas]] and [[notes]] to achieve deeper [[discovery]]. By fostering a community where participants can engage directly with one another, we move beyond solitary production into a richer, interconnected [[environment]].

> [!idea]
> True collaboration is not merely working together; it is the ==synchronization of curiosity== between independent thinkers, enabled by shared tools that support the free flow of thought.

Ultimately, collaboration requires a foundation of [[sovereignty]], ensuring that while we build together, each individual retains the agency to shape their own [[workspace]] and [[workflow]].
