A ==mutation== is the fundamental building block of an [[item]]'s existence. Rather than viewing data as a static snapshot, we define an item by the sequence of transformations applied to it over time. By capturing every creation, update, or relation as a discrete change log, we gain total clarity into the evolution of our digital objects. This approach enables us to reconstruct an item’s state at any point in history, fostering a more transparent, predictable, and malleable computing [[environment]].

> [!idea] The history of an item is as valuable as its current state. By treating changes as first-class citizens, we move toward an [[os]] where data is inherently temporal and self-documenting.

This method deeply intersects with how we manage our [[workflow]], ensuring that [[navigation]] through our digital life is grounded in the reality of its own creation and continuous development.
