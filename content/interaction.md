# interaction

[[interaction]] is the vital bridge between human [[intent]] and digital [[environment]], a constant, unfolding conversation rather than a rigid set of commands. By focusing on how we engage with [[cognition]] through our tools, we move toward a more fluid [[interface]] where the machine acts as an extension of the self. This active process requires us to rethink how we perform [[actions]] within our software, ensuring that every touchpoint fosters [[creativity]] rather than creating friction.

> [!idea]
> True interaction is not about using software; it is about ==thinking through the computer==.

When we treat the machine as a collaborative partner, we unlock new levels of [[agency]], allowing our digital [[assets]] to exist in a persistent, accessible state that mirrors our own evolving thought processes.
