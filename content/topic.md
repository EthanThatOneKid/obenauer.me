Topics provide a fluid mechanism for structuring our [[workspace]], enabling the collection of any [[item]]—regardless of type—into a meaningful [[organization]] or [[graph]]. By moving beyond rigid silos, we embrace a system where a single entity can inhabit multiple contexts simultaneously, fostering organic discovery and intuitive [[organization]].

> [!idea]
> The power of topics lies in their flexibility; they allow us to pin references and cultivate domains of thought that evolve alongside our [[project]].

By leveraging these non-exclusive groupings, we achieve a more human-centric [[architecture]] of information, turning static data into active, interconnected knowledge. This promotes true [[sovereignty]] over how we relate to our digital assets.
