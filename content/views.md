A [[views|view]] is a definition that mediates how we interact with [[items]]. By treating these definitions as items themselves, we transform the [[interface]] into a flexible, user-defined layer rather than a fixed container. This approach creates a [[metadata]] UI language, decoupling the user's intent from the underlying [[software]] stack or platform. In this vision, we move toward a [[sovereignty]] where the environment adapts to the user, allowing us to swap, construct, and refine how information is rendered and manipulated with profound malleability.
