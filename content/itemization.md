# itemization

[[itemization]] is the fundamental shift toward a personal [[computing]] environment where data is decoupled from the monolithic [[software]] containers we currently call apps. By decomposing our digital world into atomic, independent units, we create a system where items can freely reference one another, fostering a more fluid [[environment]].

> [!important]
> In an itemized system, items exist independently of the views that render them. This separation allows for highly adaptable hierarchies and perspectives, empowering users to move from passive consumers to active architects of their own digital tools.

> [!idea]
> By removing the friction of app-bound silos, we provide individuals the space to organize their information in ways that mirror their unique thought patterns, turning the computer into a medium that truly amplifies human potential.
