# research

Research is the act of advancing humanity's collective knowledge by exploring what we do not yet know, fundamentally driven by [[curiosity]] and deep personal engagement. Unlike the structured, known-path application of [[engineering]], research occupies the space of uncertainty where the outcome is not guaranteed, demanding that we constantly rethink our [[computing]] tools and the very [[environment]] in which we create. It is a commitment to learning everything that has come before, only to then push beyond those boundaries to reveal something entirely new.

> [!idea]
> To do great research is to be willing to work in the unknown; if you already know exactly what you are doing, you are likely engineering, not researching.

==Real research is transformative==, changing not only the subject of study but the researcher themselves, moving them from passive observers to active participants in the expansion of what is possible.
