In an [[itemized]] environment, a ==node== acts as a fundamental unit of [[cognition]] and representation, serving as a primary building block within a [[graph]]-based [[architecture]]. These nodes exist as distinct, granular [[items]] that prioritize [[interoperability]], allowing users to form complex, meaningful [[relationships]] through [[linking]] and [[association]]. By shifting away from rigid file-based structures toward a fluid, [[malleable]] [[system]], nodes empower us to externalize our [[thinking]] process, transforming static data into a dynamic, interconnected [[workspace]] that expands human [[agency]] and [[creativity]].
