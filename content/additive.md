To be [[additive]] is to exist within a medium that appreciates over [[time]],
contributing to the [[knowledge]] of human [[knowledge]] rather than succumbing
to the inevitable depreciation of modern [[software]]. While traditional
creative works—books, film, music—naturally persist and gain value across
generations, most digital [[tool|tools]] are ephemeral, requiring constant
maintenance and reconstruction just to remain functional. True [[thinking]]
requires an [[environment]] where our efforts aren't lost to the sands of the
[[timeline]]; it demands we build in ways that ensure our digital contributions
retain their [[sovereignty]] and utility long after the original [[context]] of
their [[creation]] has passed.

> [!note] Essential Insight [[software]] today is often subtractive: it demands
> constant labor to exist in the present. We must strive for ==additive
> [[system|systems]]== that allow our work to live on in its own right, becoming
> part of a permanent, evolving human heritage.

My [[research]] is dedicated to this [[fundamental]] shift: moving away from the
fleeting nature of current applications and toward a durable [[architecture]]
that fosters [[discovery]] and long-term [[mastery]].
