Ideation is the heartbeat of creation, a vital practice akin to a musician mastering scales. It is not merely a precursor to shipping, but a fundamental act of [[thinking]] and [[creativity]] that sharpens the mind and clarifies one's [[research]] process. By engaging in the exploration of ideas—even those deemed impossible—we cultivate the flexibility to uncover unexpected connections that infuse our work with profound novel insights.

> [!idea] Ideas are magic.
> To dismiss ideation as worthless without immediate execution is a failure to recognize the fertile ground it provides.

We must treat ideation as a dedicated craft, trusting that these moments of exploration directly inform the depth of our [[design]] and the quality of our eventual output. When we embrace the merit of every spark, we evolve how we approach problem-solving, turning simple concepts into the fire of future realities.
