[[messaging]] is a foundational layer for human connection that must be decoupled from the intrusive, attention-fragmenting patterns of modern [[os]] design. By shifting from top-down notifications to context-aware, [[itemcentric]] exchanges, we reclaim [[sovereignty]] over our focus and the flow of information across our [[workspaces]]. 

> [!idea] 
> True communication should function as a living, [[live]] stream of relevant [[items]], where [[interaction]] feels like an extension of one’s own [[cognition]] rather than an interruption by a foreign system.

We must reimagine these pathways to ensure that every exchange is grounded in the user's specific [[context]], turning what is currently a source of digital noise into a coherent, [[malleable]] thread of [[relationships]] and meaningful [[collaboration]].
