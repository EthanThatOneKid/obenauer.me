[[messaging]] is a foundational [[layer]] for human connection that must be
decoupled from the intrusive, attention-fragmenting patterns of modern [[os]]
[[design]]. By shifting from top-down [[notification|notifications]] to
[[context]]-aware, [[item|item-based]] exchanges, we reclaim [[sovereignty]]
over our focus and the flow of information across our [[workspace|workspaces]].

> [!idea] True communication should function as a living,
> [[interaction|interactive]] stream of relevant [[item|items]], where
> [[interaction|interacting]] feels like an extension of one’s own [[thinking]]
> rather than an interruption by a foreign [[system]].

We must reimagine these pathways to ensure that every exchange is grounded in
the user's specific [[context]], turning what is currently a source of digital
noise into a coherent, malleable thread of [[relationship]] and meaningful
[[collaboration]].
