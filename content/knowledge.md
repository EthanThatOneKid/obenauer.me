> [!idea] Knowledge is the ever-expanding [[graph]] of human [[thinking]], formed by connecting disparate [[graph|strands]] of inspiration into new, [[additive]] structures.

Knowledge is not a static repository but a living, [[graph|graph-based]] process. We contribute to this global [[knowledge]] by drawing upon existing [[thinking]]—spanning ancient philosophy to modern insights—and synthesizing them into novel [[representation]]s. By weaving these threads together, we create new [[thinking|conceptual]] artifacts that serve as foundations for future generations.

> [!important] The tragedy of current [[software]] is its tendency toward depreciation; it requires constant, labor-intensive updates just to remain functional. True [[knowledge]] work demands an [[additive]] medium—one that, like a book or film, appreciates and endures, allowing our contributions to remain [[sovereignty]]-aligned and accessible across time.
