title: knowledge

> [!idea] Knowledge is the ever-expanding [[graph]] of human [[thinking]], formed by connecting disparate strands of inspiration into new, [[additive]] structures.

Knowledge is not a static repository but a living, graph-based [[process]]. We contribute to this global [[knowledge]] by drawing upon existing [[thinking]]—spanning ancient philosophy to modern insights—and synthesizing them into novel [[representation|representations]]. By weaving these threads together, we create new conceptual artifacts that serve as foundations for [[future]] generations.

> [!important] The tragedy of current [[software]] is its tendency toward depreciation; it requires constant, labor-intensive updates just to remain functional. True [[knowledge]] work demands an [[additive]] medium—one that, like a book or film, appreciates and endures, allowing our contributions to remain [[sovereignty]]-aligned and accessible across [[time]].
