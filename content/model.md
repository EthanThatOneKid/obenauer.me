---
title: model
---

[[model|models]] serve as the architectural blueprint for how our [[computing]] [[environment|environments]] [[process]] and interpret information. By abstracting the structure of our digital world, they allow us to move beyond rigid, siloed [[software]] toward a more fluid [[environment]]. These frameworks enable us to treat [[logic]] and [[view]] as interchangeable [[unit|units]], shifting the burden of [[creativity]] from the machine's constraints to the user's [[intent]].
