[[model]] serve as the architectural blueprint for how our [[computing]] environments process and interpret information. By abstracting the structure of our digital world, they allow us to move beyond rigid, siloed [[software]] toward a more fluid [[environment]]. These frameworks enable us to treat logic and [[view]] as interchangeable [[unit]], shifting the burden of creativity from the machine's constraints to the user's [[intent]].
