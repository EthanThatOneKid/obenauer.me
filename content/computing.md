[[computing|Computing]] is the [[fundamental]] act of externalizing our thoughts and
cognition through digital mediums. By shifting from opaque, siloed
[[system|systems]] toward an [[item]] [[architecture]], we reclaim our
[[agency]] over the [[tool|tools]] we inhabit. It is not merely about processing
data, but about creating [[environment|environments]] that augment our capacity
for [[thinking]] and being.

> [!idea] We must transition from being mere users of [[software]] to being
> architects of our own cognitive [[environment|environments]].

> [!important] The essence of [[computing]] lies in how we represent, store, and
> manipulate the atomic [[unit|units]] of our digital lives, transforming
> computers into extensions of our own minds.
